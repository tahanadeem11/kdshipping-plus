Why Choose KD Shipping Plus for Postcards?

We combine high-speed production with local expertise. We know the Keller area and can help you optimize your postcard design for maximum local impact.

Our most popular sizes are 4" x 6" (standard), 5" x 7", and 6" x 9" (jumbo), all of which meet USPS mailing standards.

We typically use 14pt or 16pt ultra-thick cardstock to ensure your postcards are sturdy enough to withstand the mailing process.

Yes, we are experts in EDDM. We can help you design, print, and bundle your postcards according to USPS requirements for target-route mailing.

For standard bulk orders, we typically have a 2-4 business day turnaround, depending on the finishing options you choose.
